What do we do?

LambdaClass specializes in technologies centered on data engineering, ranging from building specialized infrastructure and distributed systems ensuring visibility and fault tolerance, data pipelines equipped to handle, process and analyze big data, and the data science and statistical analysis skills to gain answers to your business questions.

Who are we?

We are a collective of passionate engineers with a strong focus on technical profiles. We specialize in solving advanced technical challenges for companies that are scaling up and innovating. We constantly research new technologies that expand the boundaries of possibility for our clients. We post about our latest interests in our blog, and we stay in touch with the community by organizing monthly meetups and two annual conferences, Buzzconf and Zruput.

How do we do it?

We are client oriented engineers; we work side by side with your team, providing solutions and answers to your technical needs. We adapt to your workflow, and we believe that open communication with aligned expectations is the most efficient way of working.

Why work with us?

Experienced team

We are not an agency. We are a highly experienced technical team that works remotely and loves new challenges. Have a problem that needs a solution? Don't hesitate to tell us about it!

Focus on Customer

We know how important it is to integrate with your team seamlessly and communicate with ease in order to work effectively. Our timezone aligns with most of the working hours in the US and Europe, allowing us to be present when you need us.
